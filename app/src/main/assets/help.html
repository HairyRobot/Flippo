Flippo Help

Flippo is a simple puzzle, the object is to flip the tiles until only the central tile is red.

You can only flip tiles in certain combinations as follow.

Assume we number the tiles like this:

1 2 3
4 5 6
7 8 9

If you flip any corner tile, all neighbouring tiles are flips. i.e. flipping tile 1, flip 2, 4 and 5 as well.

If you flip the middle tile of the top or bottom row, the whole row flips. i.e. flipping tile 8, flip 7 and 9 as well.

If you flip the middle tile of the leftmost or rightmost column, the whole column flips. i.e. flipping tile 4, flip 1 and 7 as well.

If you flip the central tile 5, tiles 2, 4, 6 and 8 are also flips.

In principle any board should be solvable in at most 9 flips.

There are 511 board combinations (127 unique combinations), can you solve them all in 9 flips of less.

Scoring system

The fewer flips used to solve a board, the higher the score.
